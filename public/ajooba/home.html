About Us

The team of FreeEarth consists of 5 like-minded individuals who share a common goal of spreading awareness about how human activity has affected the environment and propose measures that can be taken to prevent it. Each member of the team comes from various third-world countries which have all been impacted changes in the environment. The team acknowledges that climate change has impacted the quality of living and aspires to do something to promote better living. Each member is equipped with diverse skillsets which when brought together will result in the success of the project
